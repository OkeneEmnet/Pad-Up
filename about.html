About

In a culture of menstrual misinformation and societal taboos, Pad Up is a welcome resource/Shop for girls, women, and parent alike from eco-friendly products to weekly educational chats on our social media platforms. Every Facet of Pad Up is designed to inform and empower.

Pad Up is inspired by helping younger girls and women get prepared for their period, be it a first timer or advanced in period. Pad Up helps women pack up the essential tools/cares needed for their period days, these period boxes comes in five sets, which includes: Premium Package, Gold Package, Silver package, Bronze package and the survival package.

Each period kit includes a sanitary pad, panty liners, antiseptic liquid, roll on, mild soap, wipes, snacks and a disposable bag.

Our period kit is good for you, and good for the earth, we've sourced the healthiest products around, helping reduce the waste and negative side effects of traditional products.

Our kit ensures that you're never caught without the product you need when Aunt flow shows up on your doorstep. Toss it in your backpack, your bag, or your desk so you're always prepared,

Our kits are designed to be reused time and time again. when you run out, shop our online store and fill it back up again.

Recieve Instant access to our self-care-guide, a step by step pinkprint for crafting healthier, happier life. we share our best tips and tricks to help you go with our flow. *Check our social media platforms for more tips*
